About Me

I'm a coach, an engineer, and an expat, but more than any title, I'm someone driven by a deep commitment to helping others grow and thrive.

  • Early career

    Over 15 years, I've led numerous projects focused on improving and developing systems within global organizations. What truly energized me wasn't just the innovation, it was the connection and collaboration with people from all walks of life. That human element became not just an ingredient of success, but the foundation of my approach to coaching today.

  • Coaching career

    My coaching journey began when I started exploring how to truly connect with others and communicate with intention. What began as a personal quest to understand myself turned into a transformative process, one that revealed the power of awareness and inspired me to guide others on a similar path.

  • Life is a journey

    Self-awareness is where transformation begins. I'm here to walk beside you as you uncover your strengths and step fully into your own growth story.

Services

Life Coaching

Life coaching is a transformative process in which you engage in a deep exploration of your own life, collaborating with your coach to uncover strategies for crafting a more fulfilling, empowered, and joyful existence. Motivate yourself, your surroundings, and your team!

Performance Coaching

Performance coaching is a goal-driven process, where the coach facilitates the coachee's journey toward achieving specific objectives. This approach begins with the clear definition of a concrete goal, which serves as the foundation for the coaching process. Elevate your performance to its utmost potential!

Career Coaching

Career coaching involves a personalized, one-on-one partnership between a coach and an individual, aimed at achieving professional goals and unlocking their full potential. Unleash your potential and take decisive action!

Expat Coaching

Expat coaching is a specialized service tailored to assist expatriates in managing their professional trajectories while living and working abroad.Make deliberate progress with concrete steps towards your objective!

Others

You may have goals or concerns that differ entirely from these services, and often, something relevant may emerge during our conversations. Sessions are designed to be flexible, allowing you to focus on what feels most important to you in the present moment.

Our Process

We start with a complimentary chemistry session, a relaxed, no-pressure conversation to see if we're a good fit. You'll experience my coaching style, and I'll get to know your goals, challenges, and expectations. Mutual trust and alignment are essential, and this session helps us both make that decision with clarity.

Each session is fully focused on you, your journey, your growth, and your breakthroughs.

If we decide to move forward, we will tailor your coaching experience based on your needs and goals. The following packages are available:

  • Quick Impact (3 Sessions)

    Perfect for short-term goals, specific challenges, or focused decision-making.

  • Deep Dive (5 Sessions)

    Ideal for deeper exploration of a topic, with a manageable level of commitment.

  • Total Transformation (10 Sessions)

    Best for long-term growth, personal transformation, and momentum across multiple areas of life or work.
