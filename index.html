About Me

I am a coach, an engineer, an expat and a person who is passionate about offering heartfelt support to others.

  • Early career

    In my 15 years of professional experience, I have completed countless projects focused on process improvement, process development, and continuous improvement in global organizations, spanning various countries and encompassing diverse nationalities. Witnessing the impact of change and innovation has always excited me. Throughout these projects, the most significant element was the joy of collaborating harmoniously with people, which became one of the most essential ingredients of success.

  • Coaching career

    When I began to explore how to understand individuals, establish effective communication, and strengthen that communication even further, my coaching journey entered my life.During my coaching training, it was profoundly inspiring to see how my initial inquiry — focused on understanding and discovering myself — evolved into a deeper process of self-awareness. Gaining new insights and achievements along the way became the biggest trigger for starting my coaching career.

  • Life is a journey

    True strength begins with self-awareness.I am ready to support you on your own unique journey.Claim your role in the journey of transformation!

Why choosing coaching sessions?

A person’s values, thoughts, beliefs, and life choices shape their decisions and establish their own decision-making process. The impact and outcomes of this are connected to the relationship we have with the external world and with ourselves. In this journey, the most crucial steps for an individual are self-awareness, discovering the meaning of life, and achieving their own wholeness in order to reach their ideal version of themselves.

Services

Coaching for individuals

Life Coaching

Life coaching is a transformative process in which you engage in a deep exploration of your own life, collaborating with your coach to uncover strategies for crafting a more fulfilling, empowered, and joyful existence. Motivate yourself, your surroundings, and your team!

Performance Coaching

Performance coaching is a goal-driven process, where the coach facilitates the coachee's journey toward achieving specific objectives. This approach begins with the clear definition of a concrete goal, which serves as the foundation for the coaching process. Elevate your performance to its utmost potential!

Career Coaching

Career coaching involves a personalized, one-on-one partnership between a coach and an individual, aimed at achieving professional goals and unlocking their full potential. Unleash your potential and take decisive action!

Expat Coaching

Expat coaching is a specialized service tailored to assist expatriates in managing their professional trajectories while living and working abroad.Make deliberate progress with concrete steps towards your objective!

Others

You may have goals or concerns that differ entirely from these services, and often, something relevant may emerge during our conversations. Sessions are designed to be flexible, allowing you to focus on what feels most important to you in the present moment.
